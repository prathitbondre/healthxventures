Why Madison

Madison: Madison (and Wisconsin) has the foundation needed to be the healthcare IT hub of the US. Epic Systems is the leading healthcare EMR system in the world and located just a few miles from Madison, WI. The employees of Epic know what is needed to fix healthcare – and many of them are now starting to act on their solutions. Additionally, there are three world class healthcare systems in the Madison area – all of which are willing to try cutting edge solutions to fix healthcare. Let’s not forget one of the top public universities in the US is also located in Madison, WI. All the pieces are in place, we are just putting them into place so that soon Madison is known to the world as a place to look to for healthcare IT solutions.

Why Healthcare

Healthcare needs disruptive technologies. It’s time for the next generation of technology – there are so many ideas that need to be implemented. Caretakers need access to stored information to make better, quicker, more informed, decisions. Providers and Payers are looking for ways to reduce cost while providing better levels of care. We are confident the ideas out there and want to allow the entrepreneurs to bring these technologies to market. We want to be able to look a person in their eyes and say we are doing something. We want to help save lives. Simply put – we can do better and we will do better. Want to talk – contact us below.
