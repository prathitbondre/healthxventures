Why Madison

Madison (and Wisconsin) has the foundation needed to be the healthcare IT hub of the US. Epic Systems is the leading healthcare EMR system in the world (over 52% market share) and located just a few miles from Madison, WI. Epic employees understand the needs to fix healthcare – and many of them are now starting to create much needed solutions. Additionally, there are three world class healthcare systems in the Madison area – all of which are willing to help find solutions to fix healthcare (the hospitals were some of the earliest to implement EMR software to better healthcare). Let’s not forget one of the top public universities in the US is also located in Madison, WI. All the pieces are in place, we are just putting them into place so that soon Madison is known to the world as a place to look to for healthcare IT solutions. All the pieces are here to find world-class healthcare IT solutions.

Why Healthcare

The time is now for the next generation of health technology to be implemented. Caretakers need access to stored information to make better, quicker, and more informed decisions. Providers and payers are seeking ways to reduce costs while still providing high levels of care. Entrepreneurs have the ideas that can help make this change happen. Simply put, we want to be on the front lines helping entrepreneurs improve lives through better healthcare.
